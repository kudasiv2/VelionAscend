Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about VelionAscend

What is the minimum investment?
The minimum investment is $10 USDT. There is no maximum investment limit.
How does the ROI system work?
ROI starts at 0.5% daily and adjusts based on TVL. It increases by 0.1% for every $50,000 TVL increase, with a maximum of 2.5% daily.
When can I withdraw my profits?
You can withdraw your ROI profits at any time. There is a 10% withdrawal fee that goes back to the TVL to maintain sustainability.
How does the referral system work?
We have a 10-level referral system. You earn commissions from your referrals' withdrawals, not their deposits. Each level has specific commission rates and minimum deposit requirements.
What is the Monthly Salary?
Monthly Salary is an automatic reward paid monthly based on your team size. There are 4 levels: Pionir ($5), Visioner ($10), Arsitek ($20), and Maverick ($30) with different team requirements.
Is VelionAscend safe and secure?
Yes, VelionAscend is 100% decentralized with no admin control. The smart contract has been audited and uses ReentrancyGuard for security. All funds are stored in the contract and can only be accessed through the defined functions.
